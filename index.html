Sleep

I go to bed around 9:30 to 10:30PM and wake up around 5:30 to 6:30AM.
I tried to use some sleep trackers, but I don't like to wear anything while sleeping.
Also there is no need to test it, as I know there is need for improvement.
No point in measuring everything, when it doesn't result in any action.
I apply this principle to everything in life.
Also I limit blue light exposure to my eyes.
I were blue light blocking glasses after sunset.
And use related settings in mobile and laptop.
I avoid turning on tube lights after sunset.
If needed I use incandescent bulbs.
I do take Melatonin sometimes, I will talk about it in detail there.
